# Phoenix Home-Services Lead Gen Launch

## Objective
Build a cash-flow-first service business in Phoenix focused on lead generation, follow-up systems, missed lead recovery, and appointment setting for home-service businesses.

## Why this business
This path fits the need for faster income, strong repeatable systems, local market density, and a service model that can be operated with disciplined outreach and process.

## Best starting niche
Primary recommendation:
Pest control

Secondary option:
HVAC

## Core offer
We help home-service businesses book more qualified appointments and recover lost revenue through lead generation, follow-up systems, and missed lead recovery.

## What is being sold
More booked estimates
More qualified conversations
Faster follow-up
Less lead leakage
More pipeline visibility

## What is not being sold first
Branding
Full agency services
SEO retainers
Website rebuilds
Generic AI consulting

## Initial pricing
Suggested starting point:
$500 setup
$1,000 per month

Optional later layer:
performance bonus per qualified booked appointment

## First execution sequence
1. Choose niche and final offer language
2. Build first 50 Phoenix leads
3. Prepare outreach and follow-up scripts
4. Create simple offer page or one-page sales doc
5. Run first outreach batch
6. Handle objections and book calls
7. Close a pilot client
8. Document delivery workflow and case-study evidence

## Success criteria
First positive reply
First booked call
First pilot client
Clear repeatable outreach process

## Failure conditions
No offer clarity
No outreach volume
No follow-up discipline
Too many niches at once
Overbuilding before sales

## Operator rule
Do not expand the business model until one niche, one offer, and one outreach process are working.
